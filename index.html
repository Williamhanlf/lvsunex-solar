About LVSUNEX Solar

Excellence in DC Solar Cooling Solutions.

Established in 2015, LVSUNEX Solar is a professional high-tech enterprise dedicated to the R&D and manufacturing of high-performance 12V/24V DC solar freezers. Based in Ningbo, China, we deliver sustainable cooling solutions to over 50 countries worldwide.

Every unit undergoes 100% performance testing in extreme ambient temperatures (up to 43°C) to ensure uncompromised cooling in tropical environments. We provide comprehensive OEM/ODM services and 24/7 technical support.
