About Our Company

LVSUNEX Solar: Your Global Partner in DC Solar Cooling Excellence

Since 2015, LVSUNEX Solar has been a pioneer in manufacturing high-performance 12V/24V DC solar freezers and refrigerators. Based in Ningbo, China, we have evolved into a comprehensive industry leader integrating R&D, manufacturing, and global export.

We specialize in providing reliable off-grid cooling solutions for over 50 countries. Every LVSUNEX product features advanced DC inverter technology and superior insulation.
