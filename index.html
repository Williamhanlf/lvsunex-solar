About LVSUNEX Solar

Excellence in DC Solar Cooling Solutions.

Established in 2015, LVSUNEX Solar is a professional high-tech enterprise dedicated to the research, development, and manufacturing of high-performance 12V/24V DC solar freezers and refrigerators. Strategically located in the port city of Ningbo, China, we leverage world-class logistics and manufacturing expertise to deliver sustainable cooling solutions worldwide.

Our factory is equipped with advanced automated assembly lines and rigorous testing chambers. Every LVSUNEX unit undergoes 100% performance testing in extreme ambient temperatures (up to 43°C) to ensure uncompromised cooling performance in tropical and off-grid environments across Africa, Southeast Asia, and the Middle East.

Beyond manufacturing, we offer comprehensive OEM/ODM services and 24/7 technical support. With over a decade of export experience, LVSUNEX has become a trusted partner for international distributors, NGOs, and government energy projects.
