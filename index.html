About LVSUNEX Solar

Excellence in DC Solar Cooling Solutions.

Established in 2015, LVSUNEX Solar is a professional high-tech enterprise dedicated to the research, development, and manufacturing of high-performance 12V/24V DC solar freezers and refrigerators. Strategically located in the port city of Ningbo, China, we deliver sustainable cooling solutions worldwide.

Our factory is equipped with advanced automated assembly lines and rigorous testing chambers. Every LVSUNEX unit undergoes 100% performance testing in extreme ambient temperatures (up to 43°C) to ensure uncompromised cooling performance in tropical and off-grid environments across Africa, Southeast Asia, and the Middle East.

At LVSUNEX, we believe in Technology for Life. By integrating the latest high-efficiency DC compressors and thick-density insulation technology, our products achieve the perfect balance between low power consumption and rapid freezing, providing essential food and medical security for communities without stable grid access.
