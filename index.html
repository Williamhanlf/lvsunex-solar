About LVSUNEX Solar

Driven by Innovation, Powered by the Sun.

Established in 2015, LVSUNEX Solar is a premier high-tech enterprise dedicated to the research, development, and manufacturing of high-performance 12V/24V DC solar appliances. Based in Ningbo, China, we deliver sustainable cooling solutions worldwide.

Our factory features advanced automated assembly lines and rigorous testing chambers. Every LVSUNEX unit undergoes 100% performance testing in extreme ambient temperatures (up to 43°C) to ensure uncompromised cooling performance in tropical off-grid environments.
